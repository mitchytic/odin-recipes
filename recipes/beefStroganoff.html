Beef Stroganoff

Description

This dish is a simple combination of beef, pasta, and a creamy sauce. It is very simple process to get going, taking only 20 minutes total!

This recipe is ripped straight from here, this webpage is just for practice and I'm not whipping up a whole recipe for it.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of egg noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 can of fat free condensed cream of mushroom
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients mise en place
  2. Saute ground beef in a pan until browned, make sure to get that maillard reaction for big flavor!
  3. Fill a pot with water and a pinch of salt, then put it on the stove and bring it to a boil on high heat. After the water has boiled, cook noodles until they are at a consistency you like (suggested 7-9 minutes), after which they should be set aside.
  4. Drain and discard any excess fat from the beef, and stir in the cream of mushroom and garlic powder. Turn the heat down to low, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Remove the beef pan from heat and stir in the egg noodles from before. Add in the sour cream, salt and pepper to taste, and mix vigorously.
  6. Eat it while it's hot and enjoy!
