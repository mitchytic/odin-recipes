Spaghetti

Go Home

Description

Yummy italian noodles with tomato sauce and beef bolognese.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked spaghetti noodles
  • Beef bolognese sauce

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti noodles in a pot according to the instructions on the packaging.
  2. In a saucepan, put the heat on medium and add the desired amount of beef sauce, heating for 12 minutes.
  3. Drain the water from the boiled spaghetti, leaving half a cup of the residual spaghetti water.
  4. Mix the spaghetti water into the beef sauce, cooking for an additional 3 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Put the noodles onto a serving plate, and then pour the sauce on the noodles.
