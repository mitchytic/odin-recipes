Caprese Salad

Go Home

Description

An italian sald made of sliced tomatoes and mozzarella, garnished with basil leaves.

Ingredients

  • Fresh mozzarella
  • 1 Tomato
  • Olive oil
  • Basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Slice the mozzarella and tomato into circular pieces with even thickness.
  2. Drizzle the serving plate with a thin layer of olive oil.
  3. Stack tomatoes and mozarella slices in a spiral similar to the image.
  4. Garnish with basil evenly between the slices of tomatoes and cheese.
  5. Drizzle a layer of olive oil over the salad to finish.
