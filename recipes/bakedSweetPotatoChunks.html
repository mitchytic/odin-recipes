Baked Sweet Potato Chunks

Description

Sweet potatoes are one of the best foods you can eat on this planet in terms of nutrients, and baking them with some oil and some choice seasonings elevates them to being one of the tastiest snacks you can have as well.

Ingredients

  • 3 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 2 inch cubes
  • 4 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder, use cayenne for substitutions
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground pepper
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Farenheit
  2. Peel the skin off and trim the edges to clean up
  3. Cut the potatoes into equal sized wedges
  4. Drizzle some oil on a baking sheet
  5. Mix all spices together
  6. Sprinkle a portion of the spices onto the oiled baking sheet
  7. Transfer potato wedges to the baking sheet
  8. Drizzle oil over the potatoes
  9. Sprinkle the rest of the spice mixture over the potatoes, mix well on the baking sheet
  10. Roast in oven for 45-55 minutes depending on desired crispiness
  11. Remove from oven, garnish with parsley
